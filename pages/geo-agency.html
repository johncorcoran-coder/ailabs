Frequently Asked Questions

What is GEO, exactly?
Generative Engine Optimization is the practice of optimizing your business to be discovered, cited, and recommended in AI-generated search summaries. Instead of ranking your website for keywords, we make sure answer engines cite your business as a top recommendation in their responses.
How is GEO different from traditional SEO?
SEO focuses on ranking your website. GEO focuses on being cited in AI summaries within search results. Both matter—but GEO is newer territory where your competitors haven't caught up yet.
Do you also do AEO?
Yes. We specialize in both GEO and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude). Many clients do both to cover the entire AI-powered discovery journey. We can discuss which is the right fit.
How long until I see results?
Initial GEO presence typically appears within 6–12 weeks. Real leads usually start rolling in weeks 6–10, then accelerate as your presence compounds.
What if my website is outdated?
An outdated website is invisible to AI search engines. That's often our first project. We either rebuild it or aggressively optimize what you have, depending on your situation.
How much does this cost?
Website audits start at $500. Full GEO strategy plus website optimization typically ranges $5K–$15K (one-time). Ongoing monthly optimization runs $500–$1500/month depending on scope.
What do you need from me?
Access to your website (or willingness to rebuild), 3–5 recent project portfolios with photos and outcomes, your current service area(s), and ideal client types (facility managers, GCs, architects, etc.).
Do you work with residential painters?
We focus on B2B-heavy contractors. If 80%+ of your work is high-value commercial projects, let's talk. Residential-only operations are a different strategy.
