GEO for MSPs — FAQ

What is GEO for MSPs?
GEO for MSPs is Generative Engine Optimization specifically for managed service providers. It's the strategy of getting your MSP cited, recommended, and referenced in AI-powered search results—ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and Claude. When a business owner asks an AI "Who's the best MSP near me?", GEO ensures your company is in the answer.
How is GEO different from traditional SEO for MSPs?
Traditional SEO ranks your MSP website in Google's blue links. GEO positions your MSP to be cited and recommended inside AI-generated answers and summaries. Both matter, but GEO is the emerging channel where most MSPs have zero presence—giving early movers a massive competitive advantage.
How long does GEO take to work?
Most MSPs see initial AI search visibility within 6-12 weeks. Qualified lead flow typically begins around weeks 8-12 and accelerates as your authority compounds. Results depend on your existing online presence, competition in your market, and the scope of implementation.
