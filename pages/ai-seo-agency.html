What is an AI SEO agency?

An AI SEO agency specializes in Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)—strategies that help your content appear in AI-powered search results like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI search. Unlike traditional SEO agencies, an AI SEO agency optimizes for how AI systems discover, evaluate, and cite sources.

How is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) different from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO optimizes for Google's search algorithm to rank in blue links. Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) optimizes for AI systems like ChatGPT and Perplexity to have your content cited in AI-generated answers. Both are important—an AI SEO agency does both simultaneously.

Do I need an AI SEO agency if I already have an SEO company?

Most traditional SEO companies don't optimize for AI systems yet. An AI SEO agency like Smart Business Revolution combines Answer Engine Optimization, Generative Engine Optimization, and traditional SEO—ensuring you capture leads across all search channels: Google, AI answer engines, and emerging platforms.

How long does it take to see results from an AI SEO agency?

Most clients see initial visibility in AI systems (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude) within 4-8 weeks. Full content optimization and authority building typically shows significant results within 3-6 months. Results depend on competition, content quality, and existing domain authority.

What does an AI SEO agency actually do?

An AI SEO agency conducts an AI Search Audit, develops an AEO and GEO strategy, optimizes your content for AI discoverability, implements technical schema markup, builds authority signals, and continuously measures how you appear in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and other AI systems.

Can an AI SEO agency help with B2B lead generation?

Yes. B2B buyers use AI systems to research solutions before making decisions. An AI SEO agency positions your company to be recommended by these AI systems, driving qualified leads directly from answer engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity—where high-intent buyers search.
